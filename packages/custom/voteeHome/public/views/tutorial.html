New User Tutorial

To begin Votee, create an account by click the "Join" button on the front page. Next, enter your information to create a custom user profile. Once you have a profile, you will be able to login and create questions (as a professor) or login and vote as a student. Both students and professors have the ability to track their votes. Click on View Grades or View Courses on your student page to view your courses in progress or the scores you have achieved on previous Votee quizzes.

As a professor, create quizzes by clicking the "Create Quiz" button on the professor page. Additionally, view the current quiz or the past results by clicking on the corresponding buttons.
